What is the turnover rate calculator?
The turnover rate calculator is a tool that computes a company's employee turnover rate.
How to calculate turnover rate?
To calculate turnover rate you should use the following formula:
turnover rate = (number of employees who left / average number of employees) * 100
Why use turnover rate calculator?
The turnover rate calculator is used in order to determine a company's employee turnover rate.
